Muay Thai - Thai Boxing

Besides coding, my other favorite thing to do and passion is fighting (yes, fighting ...but only in the ring ahah).

I fell in love with the sport since the first time I step into the gym in 2016. I had my first competition in 2017 and since then I have been competing
actively and I got to become 2x Muay Thai champion. I train 5 days a week for about 2 to 3 hours, it's a sport that requires a lot dedication, focus and commitment.

Thanks to this sport I have learned not only to defend myself but to be a lot more dedicated, to work hard to achive my goals,
and that every sacrifice has it reward at the end, not only in the ring but in every area of my life.

Friday July 1st I will be fighting for another national belt in New York.

About the Sport

Muay Thai is the national sport of Thailand. This is a sport that began as a real-world fighting technique used in warfare several hundred years ago.
